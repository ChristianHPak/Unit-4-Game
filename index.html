Crystal Collector

Directions!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By click on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score with the random score. You lose the game if your total score is above the random score.

The values of the crystals are hidden from you until you click it.

Each time the game starts, the game changes the values of the crystals.
